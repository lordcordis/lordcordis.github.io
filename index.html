PlayShare

PlayShare is an iOS app designed to simplify the process of sharing physical copies of video games.

Features

Advanced Search: Search for games in the PlayShare database or import from IGDB.
Game Management: Add games to your shared list, wish list, or collection.
User Matching: Find users sharing specific games in your city.
Exchanges: Initiate and respond to exchanges with other users.
Telegram Integration: Continue communicating via Telegram after initiating an exchange.
Profile Management: Update your profile with game consoles, location, and Telegram username.
Local Games: Find users nearby who share or want the same games.
Exchange Tracker: Monitor your current, accepted, and archived exchanges in one place.
Secure Authentication: Sign up using email/password, Google, or Apple accounts. Passwords are secured using Apple Keychain.
